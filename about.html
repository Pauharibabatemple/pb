Welcome To The Official Website Of The Pauhari Baba Temple, Popularly Known As The Pauhari Baba Located In Saraiya Ratnave Azamgarh. People Of All Racial, Religious, National , Are Welcomed To Pray And Meditate Within Its Precincts.

The Pauhari baba Temple is located in the in Saraiya ratnave, Azamgarh UP . The parking facility is available for use. One can reach here by the following means.

From The Azamgarh City

Pauhari baba temple is 35 km away from Azamgarh City, a distance of approximately one hours by car or an auto rickshaw.
