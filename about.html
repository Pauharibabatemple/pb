Shri Pauhari Baba

Est 2020

Pauhari Baba Temple, nestled in the serene surroundings of Saraiya, is a sacred place dedicated to Pahuhari Baba, a revered spiritual figure known for his on peace, devotion, and self-realization. The temple attracts devotees and spiritual seekers from all over, offering a peaceful sanctuary to reflect, pray, and experience divine blessings. The temple is not just a place of worship but also a center for community, fostering a spirit of unity and compassion. It hosts regular prayers, festivals, and spiritual events that enrich the lives of those who visit. Devotees believe that the temple is a source of positive energy, guiding them on their spiritual journeys and helping them find strength and wisdom in their daily lives.

The Pauhari baba Temple is located in the in Saraiya ratnave, Azamgarh UP .

From The Azamgarh City

Pauhari baba temple is 35 km away from The Azamgarh City, A distance of approximately one hours by car or an auto rickshaw.
