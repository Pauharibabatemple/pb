About us

About us Shri Pauhari Baba

Pauhari Baba Temple, nestled in the serene surroundings of Saraiya, is a sacred place dedicated to Pahuhari Baba, a revered spiritual figure known for his on peace, devotion, and self-realization. The temple attracts devotees and spiritual seekers from all over, offering a peaceful sanctuary to reflect, pray, and experience divine blessings. The temple is not just a place of worship but also a center for community, fostering a spirit of unity and compassion. It hosts regular prayers, festivals, and spiritual events that enrich the lives of those who visit. Devotees believe that the temple is a source of positive energy.
